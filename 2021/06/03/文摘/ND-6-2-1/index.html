从中国互联网业头号公敌到“腾讯爸爸”

Why China’s Most-Hated Internet Company Decided to Play Nice

  1. 有什么业务是腾讯不做的吗？

    Is there any business that Tencent wouldn’t do?

  2. 但在当时，腾讯被中国科技产业视为头号公敌。这家公司会毫不犹豫地剽窃他人的创意，让初创公司活不下去。公司的高管在产业大会和媒体采访中屡遭质问。创业者们称它是业内最厚颜无耻的抄袭者。

    Dramatic, perhaps, but back then the Chinese technology industry considered Tencent to be Public Enemy No. 1. It wouldn’t hesitate to copy somebody else’s idea and drive the upstart out of business. Its top executives were confronted at industry conferences and in media interviews. Entrepreneurs called it the industry’s most brazen copycat.

  3. 十几年过去了，中国政府终于开始收紧该国最强大科技公司的缰绳——但这不包括腾讯，至少现在不包括。

    More than a decade later, the Chinese government is finally reining in the country’s most powerful technology companies — but not, at least for now.

  4. 如今业界不再大声疾呼打倒腾讯。事实上，腾讯在很多方面已经成为产业最大的、也是最具资金实力的啦啦队员。通过向小企业投入资金，收购竞争对手，而不是把它们逼上绝路，这家公司已经改变了自己的形象。

    The industry is no longer clamoring for somebody to take Tencent down. In fact, it has in many ways become the industry’s biggest and most deep-pocketed cheerleader. The company has managed to revamp its image by throwing money at the little guys and buying off competitors rather than driving them out of business.

  5. 当腾讯抄袭的时候，中国的科技公司没有倒下来，当腾讯把支票递过来后，他们便失去了抵抗的意志，纷纷投诚。

    China’s tech companies didn’t fall when Tencent was copying them, they lost their will to fight back and surrendered when Tencent held out a check.

  6. 阿里和腾讯都掌握大量资源，如果他们作恶的话危害都会很大。

    Both Alibaba and Tencent control a lot of online resources, both of them can cause tremendous harm to our society if they choose to do evil.

  7. 融洽的关系有助于巩固腾讯在中国的影响力。

    Cozy relationships help cement Tencent’s power in China.

  8. 腾讯在世界上是独一无二的。在许多层面上，它是真正的垄断者。它在中国发挥的影响力是Facebook、亚马逊(Amazon)、苹果(Apple)和谷歌(Google)梦寐以求的。

    There’s no company in the world like Tencent. It’s a true monopoly on many levels. It wields the kind of influence in China that Facebook, Amazon, Apple and Google can only aspire to.

  9. 腾讯一半的生命掌握在其投资的公司和合作伙伴手中。

    The half of Tencent’s life lies in the hands of its portfolio companies and partners.

  10. 因为我死了腾讯不会死，腾讯有千千万万个儿子。*(“我”指拼多多的创始人黄峥)*

    Tencent won’t die when Pinduoduo dies, because it has tens of thousands of sons.

  11. 无论腾讯的行为多么得体或谦虚，它都是一个巨大的企业集团，去年的利润为240亿美元，其中大部分用于投资。胜负由它来决定，但胜者并不总是业界最好的，从而损害了创新和效率。

    No matter how decent or humble Tencent may act, it’s a giant conglomerate with $24 billion in profit last year and spends much of it on investment. It picks winners and losers, but the winners won’t always be the best out there, thus harming innovation and efficiency.

  12. 它限制用户访问其他产品和服务。*(“它”指腾讯)*

    It limits user access to other products and services.

  13. 腾讯讨好的不只是这个行业。长期以来，它也一直试图接近政府。与有时桀骜不驯的阿里巴巴相比，腾讯长期以来一直公开强调愿意全面遵守规章制度。

    Tencent doesn’t just court the industry. It has also long tried to get close to the government. Compared with the sometimes defiant Alibaba, Tencent has long publicly underscored its willingness to comply fully with rules and regulations.
